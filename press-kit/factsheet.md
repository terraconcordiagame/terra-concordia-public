## One-Liner

> A digital eurogame where four civilizations must build, trade, explore and innovate — in harmony with a shared planet that can collapse if you push too hard.

## Game Description

Terra Concordia is a deep, fully-featured digital eurogame for 1 to 4 players. Choose from four distinct civilizations — Solaris (militant builders), Verdantis (ecologists), Mariteia (seafarers), or Aurífera (master traders) — and develop your people across 6 to 12 rounds.

Build from 142 hand-crafted cards: 20 Buildings, 24 Technologies, 8 Wonders, 15 Events, plus Trade, Exploration, Influence, Workers and Legacy. Manage the Global Sustainability meter: cooperate to keep the world thriving, or collapse and lose together.

The game ships with six solo scenarios (★ to ★★★★★), six variants (Quick, Standard, Epic, Beginners, Diplomacy, Solo), five included manuals, a complete Civilopedia with 142 cards, and a 161-asset Print & Play kit for the table.

## Key Features

- **142 hand-crafted cards** across 12 categories

- **30+ unique animations** and polished UX
- **19 customizable settings** (colorblind, font size, animation speed, language)
- **5 included manuals + Civilopedia + Print & Play kit**
- **Save anywhere, autosave per round**
- **No external dependencies** — Chromium and libs bundled via Electron 30
