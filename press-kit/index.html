Strategy Eurogame Indie 1-4 Players Windows Coming Soon

The Game

Terra Concordia is a deep, fully-featured digital eurogame of civilization for 1 to 4 players. Choose your people, develop technologies, erect Wonders — but every action affects the world's Global Sustainability. Push too hard, and everyone loses.

"A digital eurogame where four civilizations must build, trade, explore and innovate — in harmony with a shared planet that can collapse if you push too hard."

About NCB Company

NCB Company (New Cards and Board Games) is a Brazilian indie studio focused on deep, sustainable strategy games that bridge the worlds of digital and tabletop gaming. Terra Concordia is the studio's debut commercial release.

Design philosophy:

  1. Depth over breadth — fewer mechanics, mastered well
  2. Cooperation in competition — shared stakes that reward strategic empathy
  3. Bridge digital + tabletop — every game ships with a Print & Play kit
